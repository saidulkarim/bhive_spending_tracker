Privacy Policy – bHiVE Wallet

bHiVE Wallet is an offline-first personal finance application.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Local Storage

Financial records, accounts, categories, and backups remain stored locally on the user's device.

Backup Files

Users may manually export CSV backup files for their own storage or sharing purposes.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use advertising SDKs or analytics services.

Security

All data remains under the user's control.
